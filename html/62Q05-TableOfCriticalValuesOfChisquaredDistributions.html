table of critical values of chi-squared distributions


Below is a table of the critical values c of the chi-squared distribution (http://planetmath.org/ChiSquaredRandomVariable) corresponding to various degrees d of freedom (first column) and p-values p (first row in red).

These values can be related by the equation:

p=P(X≥c)  where X∼χ(d)2.
